## What is Fuelle?

Fuelle is a cross-platform meal planning and nutrition tracking app built with the same privacy-first philosophy as [Flowe](https://github.com/privacychase/flowe). You log your meals, see your calories and macros, and all your data stays on your device. No login required.

---

## Features (v0.5.0 alpha)

- 🍽️ **Meal logging** — Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
- 🔍 **USDA FoodData Central search** — accurate, government-verified nutrition data
- ⚖️ **Portion control** — specify grams before adding any food
- 📊 **Daily summary** — calories, carbs, protein, fat vs your goals
- 📅 **Day navigation** — browse past days
- 📆 **Week view** — see the whole week at a glance
- 📋 **History** — rolling log with per-day totals and 7-day averages
- 🎯 **Nutrition goals** — set your own daily calorie & macro targets
- 🔒 **Export / Import** — one-line encrypted backup code, works across all devices
- 🌙 **Dark & Light mode**

---

## Privacy

- **Zero accounts** — no signup, no login, ever
- **All data stored locally** on your device
- **Export code** — gzip + base64, paste it anywhere to back up or move data
- **No telemetry, no analytics, no ads**

Same export/import system as Flowe. Simple, portable, private.
